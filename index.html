About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Elena Cazinha and I am an easygoing, creative, and friendly person. I've been into creating craft work for as long as I can remember, from little knick knacks around the house, to bags, covers, blankets etc. In my spare time I like to create things that keep my mind at ease, full of joy and colorfulness. Even without a formal education in Art, I enjoy just creating things that make me happy. Truthfully, it started with my love for unicorns and bears. It truly has grown into things I love to share with everyone who wants to learn and participate in crafting; as I do hold crafting seminars.
